   ├─ Fixed TP at +10% from entry
   ├─ TP calculation: entry_price × 1.10

   └─ Automatic execution when hit

✅ Trailing Stop-Loss (The Innovation)
   ├─ Activation at +10% profit
   ├─ SL locked at entry_price × 1.04
   ├─ Lock-step trailing: SL = highest_price × 0.96
   ├─ For every 1% UP → SL moves 1% UP
   ├─ Maintains constant 6% gap
   └─ Protects profits while capturing trends

✅ Reversal Trading
   ├─ Triggered on first trade SL hit
   ├─ Flips option type (CALL → PUT, vice versa)
   ├─ Uses secondary capital (₹4,000)
   ├─ Same SL/TP logic applied
   └─ Automatic execution
